Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026  |  Version 1.0

Who We Are

BSA (Business Support Africa) is an entrepreneurship support organisation dedicated to transforming innovative ideas into high-growth, sustainable businesses. Our registered address and data controller contact details are available upon request at privacy@bsa.org.

Information We Collect

  • Name, email address, and organisation when you request an investor briefing
  • Business performance data submitted through the Swara platform
  • Correspondence and communications with our team
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, pages visited (anonymised analytics)

Legal Basis for Processing

  • Contract: Processing necessary to deliver our programmes and services
  • Legitimate Interests: Improving our platform, security monitoring, communications
  • Consent: Marketing communications (opt-in only, withdrawable at any time)
  • Legal Obligation: Compliance with applicable law and regulatory requirements

How Long We Keep Data

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purpose it was collected. Programme performance data is retained for 7 years for audit and impact reporting purposes. Contact and correspondence data is retained for 3 years after last interaction.

Your Rights

  • Right to access a copy of your personal data
  • Right to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten") where applicable
  • Right to restrict or object to processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any right, email privacy@bsa.org. We respond within 30 days.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share data with trusted sub-processors (cloud hosting, analytics) who are contractually bound to our security and privacy standards. Impact data may be shared in aggregated, anonymised form with programme funders and investors.

International Transfers

Where data is transferred outside your jurisdiction, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including standard contractual clauses where required.

Contact & Complaints

For privacy questions: privacy@bsa.org. You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority if you believe your rights have been violated.
